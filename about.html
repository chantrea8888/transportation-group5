About Transportation

What is Transportation?

Transportation is the movement of people and goods from one place to another. It plays an important role in daily life by helping people travel, work, study, and trade.

Types of Transportation

Land Transportation

  • Car
  • Bus
  • Train
  • Motorcycle

Air Transportation

  • Airplane
  • Helicopter

Water Transportation

  • Boat
  • Ship
  • Ferry

Importance of Transportation

  • Helps people travel to work and school
  • Moves goods and products
  • Supports business and economy
  • Connects cities and countries

Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages: Saves time, makes life easier, connects people.

Disadvantages: Traffic, pollution, and accidents.
